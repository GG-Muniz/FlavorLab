Mediterranean Quinoa Power Bowl

⚡ Energy Boosting ⏱️ 30 minutes 👥 4 servings 🌱 Vegetarian

Ingredients

Quinoa 1 cup Protein
Chickpeas 1 can Fiber
Spinach 2 cups Iron
Cherry tomatoes 1 cup
Cucumber 1 medium
Feta cheese ½ cup
Olive oil 3 tbsp Omega-9
Lemon 1 whole

Instructions

Rinse quinoa thoroughly under cold water. In a medium pot, combine quinoa with 2 cups of water and a pinch of salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.

While quinoa cooks, drain and rinse the chickpeas. Pat them dry with a paper towel. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.

Add chickpeas to the skillet with a pinch of salt, pepper, and your choice of spices (cumin and paprika work well). Sauté for 5-7 minutes until golden and slightly crispy.

Dice the cucumber and halve the cherry tomatoes. Wash and roughly chop the spinach if using large leaves.

Prepare the dressing by whisking together remaining olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.

Once quinoa is cooked, fluff with a fork and let it cool slightly. In serving bowls, layer quinoa, spinach, chickpeas, cucumber, and tomatoes.

Top with crumbled feta cheese and drizzle with the lemon dressing. Garnish with fresh herbs if available. Serve immediately or refrigerate for a refreshing cold salad.
